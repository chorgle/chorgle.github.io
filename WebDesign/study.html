Q: What is the Fundamental Attribution Error?


A: Our tendency to blame outside factors for ourselves, while blaming inside factors for others.

Q: What did Goffman theorize about how we act?


A: We're always acting in the role we think we're supposed to be in; that you're never really your true self.

Q: What is your Id?


A: Our initial impulse or urge when we are making decisions, usually is irrational and aggresive.

Q: When we are in like-minded groups, our beliefs tend to be


A: Reinforced through Group Polarization.

Q: What is bipolar disorder?


A: Having very high highs and very low lows emotionally.

Q: What is bipolar disorder commonly confused with?


A: Borderline Personality Disorder (BPD).

Q: When we feel uncomfortable about our behavior, we often change our attitudes to match our behavior, what is this called?


A: Cognitive Dissonance.

Q: What are the four steps of memory?


A: Sensory, Short-Term, Long-Term, and Working memory.
